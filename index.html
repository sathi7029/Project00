Achievements

Sharma was named the ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year in 2019 and was included in the ICC Men's ODI Team of the Year in 2014 and in each year from 2016 to 2019. He was also included in the ICC Men's Test Team of the Year in 2021, mainly for his achievements on India's Test tour of England that year.
